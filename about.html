About Me

My name is Rocky, and I'm a game developer from Ontario Canada. My main specialization in games, is game programming.

I've picked up games when I was younger, playing early strategy games with my family, like Rise of Nations and Stronghold Crusader. Total Annihilation, and Age of Empires were also games in the mix. This early adoption of games when I was young, sparked a passion for gaming that continues to grow today.

As a hobby, throughout middle school and highschool, I began developing games. Creating different projects, with a wonder of how developers created games, a question of how? That is the moment I decided to take my game development journey seriously, as I pursue a career in game development.

With a passion for gaming, and the process of making games, I hope to create enjoyable experiences for anyone who stumbles by my games. I appreciate the read, and take care.
